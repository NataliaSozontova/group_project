ABOUT UAE

Discover the extravagant luxury, high-tech facilities and old world charm of Dubai.
This United Arab Emirates (UAE) state is located on the eastern coast of the Arabic Peninsular,
in the southwest corner of the Arabian Gulf. Renowned, worldwide,
as a booming centre of international trade and innovation,
Dubai has fast become one of the world’s most popular tourist destinations.
Only in Dubai can one gaze upon, and travel up, the world’s largest freestanding structure (the Burj Khalifa),
shop in both exclusive designer malls and traditional trade souks and indulge in feasts
inspired by the flavours of the world while staying in a 7-star hotel!
